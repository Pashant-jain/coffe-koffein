FAQ's

Products

We will be using all sort of vegetables in our cooking. We aim to explore a variety of vegetables and spices. 

Menu

Each day, there will be two side dishes given for lunch and one side dish for dinner. Variety of menu rotated on a daily basis. Our weekly menu will be shared on every Saturday or Sunday. Our cuisine includes South Indian and North Indian.

Delivery

Charges mentioned above are inclusive of Delivery and Tax. There is no minimum order requirement. We deliver on all days (Monday to Sunday). Everyday, the delivery will be made between 8 am to 10 am.

Packaging

The dishes will be packed and sealed in compostable covers.

Customization

Since the food is cooked in bulk, personal customization would not be possible.

Payments

Payments can be made via - UPI and IMPS methods. Subscription payments are required to be paid in advance. If you wish to cancel the plan, we shall refund the balance amount.
